America Universalis Dev Diary 1

By: MultiDream


Introduction:

Hello fellow EU4 fans! My name is MultiDream, I'm a college undergraduate studying Computer Science. Eu4 is one of my favorite games right now, and after having gone through an exceptionally difficult Aztec Game, I was inspired to create this mod, America Universalis, to tip the balance oh so in America's favor. America universalis is my first mod ever published to the steam workshop. I'll be working on this mod mostly alone, so while I will try to make the most fun product possible, I can't promise preserving the game's historical accuracy. I will try to imagine the world as it could have been, had america been as advanced as europe and vice versa. While I have some basic knowledge of these regions, this is certainly not my expretise. I'd also like to note that without play testing, this mod is especially prone to bugs. While I appreciate all reports, I simply can't respond to everything, so please only refer directly to me if said bug litterally breaks the game. With that out of the way, lets move on to goals!


Goals:

My hope for this mode is to create a universe in which America is technologically Advanced, and Europe is not. Currently, I have no lore for this universe, but I am open to suggestions from the community.


This mod will attempt to hold internal historical accuracy. In essence, the timeline from start to finish should feel resonable, despite the years prior being a bafflinng mystery.

Most importantly, this mod is intended to be fun enough for at least a few campaigns, in the changed regions.America should be just as easy, if not easier, to play than in a normal Europe game. Vice versa, Europe should be just as difficult as an American Game.

I will not that this mod will probably significantly impact events. While I'll try to be careful in uprooting events in the base game, the volume of that data is so large, I can't make promises. What I can promise is new events for America and Europe, to come in a post Beta release.

Release Scheduale

Unfortunetly for any subscribers, these mods will release pretty unregularly. I'll try to make a basic HTML document Dev Diary type thing to give people an overview of whats going on, but no promises.

Still, I have all the major versions up to the 0.8 beta release planned out, as can be seen in developper files, and I'll list the goals for each patch upto then.

Sprints/Timeline:

Pre-Beta 0.1: American Religions

This release was completed June 3rd. Removes the reformable portion of the native faiths. This will allow them to accept institutions.

Pre-Beta 0.2: TradeNodes

This release was completed June 10th. Reroutes trade nodes to flow ultimately to American nodes.

Pre-Beta 0.3: American Tech

Reworks american technology groups, to make them similar to european groups.

Pre-Beta 0.4: European Tech

Reworks European technology groups. Current thought is that it will not introduce the primitive modifier, but I have yet to make the final decision on that.

Pre-Beta 0.5: Old World

Drastically reduces the size of European nations, lower devlopment significately, making it an easy target for annexation. Several Tags will be removed or kept as placeholders until their replacement can be arranged.

Pre-Beta 0.6: Institutions

Moves the Feudal institution out of Europe into America, and makes their spawning far less centralized. This will allow Asia and America to develop them somewhat separately.

Pre-Beta 0.7: Ages

Changes ages and their goals to more closely align with the American focus. Nations that gain a special boost will be more equally spread across the world.

Beta 0.8: New World

Drastically increase the amount of development in America. Increase the size of many nations, and add several new tags.

This release will constitute the minimal goal for this project. At this point, I do not promise any further development, but I do plan to maintain the project through each update.

Beta 0.9: Event Purge

Remove many of the European based events. This may speed the game up, and should fix issues from removing a large portion of Europe.

Beta 0.10: European Religions

Consolidates Abrahamic religions and lowers their influence drastically. Adds new Polystheistic religions to Europe. They may have a reform mechanic, I haven't decided.

Beta 0.11: Events and Lore

Adds many American Based Events and lore for users to enjoy.

From here on, I've not decided a path for this project. Most Likely, Ill work on each continent individually, and polish the mod until I consider it fully stand alone, then make the 1.0 release.
